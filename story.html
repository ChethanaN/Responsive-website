Story Development

TargetEnglish

1.An old woman---greedy---a goose lying a golden egg everyday---sold earned her
livelihood --not contented---all of a sudden rich---thought of hundreds of golden eggs----in
its stomach---killed---got only one---moral.

Ans: Once upon a time there lived an old woman. She had a goose. She was very greedy.
The goose laid daily one ‘golden egg’. She sold it and got money. It was her livelihood. But
she was not satisfied. She wanted to become rich at once. She thought of hundreds of
golden eggs in its stomach. So she killed the goose to get those golden eggs but she got only
one. Thus she lost her goose. Moral: Haste is waste or satisfy by what you have.

2.In a village---a clever elephant---river nearby---go for bath daily---passing by a tailor
shop---banana daily. One day not given banana---pricked with needle---elephant angry----
came with muddy water---sprayed---shop---dirty---he repented.

Ans: Once in a village there was a clever elephant .Daily the elephant goes for a bath. On its
way there was a tailors shop. Daily the tailor gives it a banana. But one day the tailor didn’t
give it a banana and he pricked its trunk with needle. The elephant got angry and brought
muddy water in its trunk. It sprayed to tailor’s shop. The shop became dirty and muddy.
After this incident he repented for his deed. Moral: Do not harm animals and make
friendship with them

3.A farmer---three sons---quarrel---decides to teach a lesson---gives them bundle of
sticks to break---could not break. Then asked to untie---could break----moral.

Ans:A former had three sons. They didn’t work in the fields and wasted time in quarrelling
each other. The father advised them but they didn’t listen his words. When the father was in
his death bed he wanted to teach a lesson to them. He called them near and gave a bundle
of sticks and asked them to break it. All tried but failed to break. Then farmer asked them to
untie the bundle and break. They untied and broke sticks easily. Then farmer advised them
to live in unity. Moral: Unity is strength.

4.An ant ---walking ---stream---fell---a dove saw it. Pity---gave leaf---the ant climbed---
banks---thanked. A hunter---aimed at dove---ant---bit---missed the aim---hunter ran away—
dove thanked--- Moral.

Ans:Once an ant walking on the banks of stream but suddenly fell into the stream. The ant
was trying to hold leaf or twig but failed. A dove was watching the ant from the branch of
tree. It felt pity on him and it took leaf and dropped it near the ant. The climbed on to the
leaf. The wind drove the leaf to the banks of the river. The ant was saved. It thanked to
dove. One day a hunter came for hunting and he saw a dove and aimed it to shoot. The ant
saw it. It quickly came to the hunter and bit his toe. He missed his aim and ran away. The
dove thanked ant. Moral: A friend in need is a friend indeed.

5.Three friends--two geese, a tortoise--decide to migrate—food, water--geese fly-- tortoise
can’t---devise a way---carry tortoise---hold stick with their beaks—tortoise not to speak—
hold the middle of the stick—children clap—tortoise angry—bursts out—fell—
moral.

Ans:Once there were three friends two geese and a tortoise. They decided to migrate in
search of food and water. The geese were able to fly but tortoise can’t. They devised a way
to carry tortoise. They held a stick with their beaks and said tortoise to hold the middle of
the stick with its mouth and not to speak. Geese carried tortoise and flew. Children saw that
and started to clap. Tortoise became angry, it bursts out and fell down.Moral: Always be
calm and not to burst out without any reason.

6.A crow—very thirsty—search of water—but not find water—it sat on a tree—saw a pot--
-flied towards pot—water was at bottom level—unable to drink—thought of to put stones—
it took stones one by one—started putting—water raised—drank water—flew away—moral.

Ans:Once there was a crow. He was very thirsty. He went in search of water but didn’t find
water. He became sad and sat on a tree. He saw a pot nearby and flied towards it. But water
was at the bottom level, so he was unable to drink the water. He thought of a plan to put
stones into the pot. He took stones one by one and started putting into it. Water level raised
he drank water and flew away. Moral: Where there is a will there is a way
